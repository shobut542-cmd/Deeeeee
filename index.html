Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer painless dental treatments?

Yes! We use modern anesthesia techniques and gentle procedures to ensure a comfortable, near-painless experience for every treatment, including root canals and extractions.

How much do dental implants cost in Ludhiana?

Implant costs vary based on the case and material used. Book a free consultation with Dr. Palak and we'll provide a detailed, transparent treatment plan and quote.

Do you treat children?

Absolutely. Our pediatric dentistry services are designed to make children feel safe and comfortable, helping build positive dental habits from a young age.

We are open Monday to Saturday from 10:00 AM to 8:00 PM. Sunday appointments are available on request — just message us on WhatsApp.

Do you accept walk-in appointments?

While we recommend booking in advance to minimize wait time, we do accommodate walk-ins based on availability. Call ahead to confirm.
